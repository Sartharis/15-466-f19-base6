Harvest Island Presskit

Description

Harvest Island is a game about farming magical plants. Fulfill magical plant orders to unlock more plants and get money. Your goal is to unlock all of the plants without becoming bankrupt. Use the magical plants' special effects to your benefit and carefully plan out your farm to reach your goal!


Harvest Island is available for Windows, Mac and Linux.


Team
This game was created over the course of 5 weeks by Rain Du, Lexi Luo, Jan Orlowski, and Margot Stewart as a final project for Carnegie Mellon University's 15-466 course.
