Manage the magical farm, fulfill orders, and redeem your lost soul from the evil island god.
Available on Windows, Mac and Linux.

Grow and harvest plants for sale or to unlock more plants.

Use the magical plants' special effects to your benefit.

Slowly expand the farm but avoid bankrupcy.
