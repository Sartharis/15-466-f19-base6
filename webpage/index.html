Manage the magical farm, fulfill orders, and redeem your lost soul from the evil island god.

Basic control:

  • Left click: interact with UI.
  • WASD: move the camera.

    • Advanced:

  • 1, 2, 3, 4 or scrollwheel: switch tools.
  • Right click: select default tool (glove).
  • TAB: open or close magic book.
  • Q, E: flip pages in magic book.
